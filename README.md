# Phishing Email Detection & Awareness System

## Overview

This project focuses on identifying and analyzing phishing emails to improve cybersecurity awareness. The objective is to detect phishing indicators, classify suspicious emails, and educate users about common phishing techniques and prevention measures.

## Objective

* Analyze phishing email samples
* Identify phishing indicators
* Classify emails as phishing or suspicious
* Understand email-based cyber threats
* Promote cybersecurity awareness and safe email practices

## Tools Used

* GitHub
* Public Phishing Email Datasets
* Email Header Analysis
* Browser Tools

## Phishing Indicators Identified

During the analysis, the following phishing indicators were observed:

* Different sender and reply-to email addresses
* Failed SPF authentication
* Suspicious domains
* Encoded or misleading subject lines
* Fake security notifications
* Impersonation of trusted organizations

## Prevention & Awareness Guidelines

### Do's

* Verify sender email addresses
* Check domains carefully
* Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
* Report suspicious emails
* Verify requests through official channels

### Don'ts

* Do not click unknown links
* Do not download suspicious attachments
* Do not share passwords or OTPs
* Do not trust urgent messages blindly

## Conclusion

The analyzed emails contained multiple phishing indicators including spoofed sender identities, failed authentication checks, suspicious domains, and deceptive messaging techniques. Users should remain vigilant and follow cybersecurity best practices to protect themselves from phishing attacks.
